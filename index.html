What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence, or AI, is like teaching computers to think and learn like humans do. Instead of just following strict rules, AI systems can look at lots of information, find patterns in it, and make smart decisions based on what they've learned.

AI learns from data - which is just information like pictures, numbers, words, or sounds. The more good quality data it sees, the better it gets at recognizing patterns and making predictions, kind of like how you get better at video games the more you play!

AI in Your Daily Life

📺

YouTube Recommends Videos

YouTube's AI watches what videos you click, like, and watch all the way through. It uses this data to predict what you'll enjoy next and shows those videos on your homepage.

📱

Phone Unlocks with Your Face

Your phone creates a detailed 3D map of your face using thousands of invisible dots. When you look at it, the AI compares your live face to this map to verify it's really you.

🌍

Duolingo Adapts to Your Level

Duolingo's AI tracks which words you get right or wrong and how fast you answer. It adjusts lesson difficulty in real-time, giving you more practice on weak areas and skipping what you've mastered.
